Tuples: Advanced Examples

Employee Record

          
  -- An employee record using a tuple
  employeeRecord = ("John Doe", 30, "Software Engineer", 85000.50)

Nested Tuples for Departments

          
  -- Nested tuple representing a department
  departmentRecord = ("IT Department", ("John", 30), ("Jane", 28), ("Alice", 35))

Lists: Advanced Examples

Shopping Cart

          
  -- A shopping cart
  shoppingCart = ["Apples", "Bananas", "Cherries", "Dates"]
  newCart = shoppingCart ++ ["Eggs"]

Gradebook

          
  -- A gradebook with student names and scores
  gradebook = [("Alice", 95), ("Bob", 85), ("Charlie", 78)]

Combining Tuples and Lists

Student Details

          
  -- Student details with tuples and lists
  students = [("Alice", 21, [85, 90, 95]), ("Bob", 22, [78, 80, 82])]
